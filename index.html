The couple

Our love story began in Reykjavik, back in 2015. Emilie just moved to Iceland to begin to work for a travel company. Sigurdur was her colleague and first host, and the magic was about to happen…

Some flirtatious banter was exchanged back and forth in the office and afterwork over a drink or a pool for weeks after Emilie moved to her apartment. The rest is history!

4 years worth of memories later, our family got bigger from four people with our lovely Ivar and Heiddis, to five as we welcome into our lives our precious son Odinn.

A few months ago, Sigurdur finally popped the question on a normal day at home and Emilie said Yes!

We can’t wait for this exciting new chapter to begin!
